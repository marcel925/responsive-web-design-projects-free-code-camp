Arnold Schwarzenegger

Arnold Schwarzenegger Champion BodyBuilder

Arnold rose to the top and conquered 4 different fields.

First: Arnold conquered bodybuilding.

He moved from Austria to California and became the youngest ever Mr Olympia Champion, aged 23. He went on to become a 7 time Mr Olympia Champion and a 4 time Mr Universe Winner.

Second: Arnold conquered the business world.

By the age of 30, Schwarzenegger was a millionaire, well before his career in Hollywood. He started with a bricklaying business. He used the profits from that to start a mail order business. Then, he invested those profits in real estate and other investments.

Third: Arnold conquered Hollywood.

He struggled at the beginning and was told many times that he should give up because his accent was too strong and his body was too big. He used these to his advantage and became the highest paid actor in 1991. His film "Terminator 2: Judgement Day" grossed over $500 million. He was a 1977 Golden Globe Award winner and he has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

Fourth: Arnold conquered politics.

In 2013, he became the governor of California, the higest attainable position in politics for a person who wasn't born in the United States.

"Money doesn't make you happy. I now have $50 million, but I was just as happy when I had $48 million" Arnold Schwarzenegger
